Factions

A simple guide to the three factions and how they counter each other.

The Three Factions

  • Blood Rose (red)
  • Wings of Dawn (blue)
  • Guard of Order (orange)

Use the colors to remember each faction quickly when building or reviewing teams.

Faction Relationship (Rock-Paper-Scissors)

  • Red beats orange.
  • Blue beats red.
  • Orange beats blue.

This page covers only the basic matchup relationship, so you can learn the foundation first.
