How Hero Battlefield Works

Hero Battlefield has three battlefields, one for each faction: Blood Rose, Wings of Dawn, and Guard of Order.

The battlefields reset every 2 days, so you can replay levels or push further on the next cycle.

Each battlefield should be cleared with its matching faction. Because faction matchups matter, keep teams aligned by faction.

Need a quick faction refresher? Read the Factions page.

Important: once a hero is used in one battlefield, that hero is locked and cannot be used in the other battlefield paths until reset. To avoid getting stuck, do not mix teams across factions.
