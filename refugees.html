Refugees & Recruitment

Refugees are support units you can recruit through the Refugee Card system. Each run shows 3 refugees with different jobs and qualities. Knowing when to recruit, when to reroll, and how weighted odds work helps you spend tickets efficiently.

  • Refresh is also free after 12 hours or at daily reset (midnight).
  • Pity guarantees an Orange appears after 10 recruitments.

How Weighted Recruiting Works

  • Each run shows 3 refugees.
  • Recruiting is not flat once they are shown.
  • The game picks from the currently shown, unrecruited refugees using weights.

Chance to recruit a shown refugee = that refugee's weight / total weight of all unrecruited refugees shown.

  • Orange / S = weight 1
  • Purple / Blue / Green = weight 2

Because Orange has lower weight than other qualities, it is naturally harder to pull on a fresh board unless weaker options have already been removed.

Strategy for Targeting Orange/Purple

  • If a board shows no Orange/Purple, reroll.
  • If a board shows Orange/Purple, the 500-ticket first recruit is usually worth taking.
  • If the first recruit misses, do not automatically buy the 2000 second recruit.
  • Main exception: buy the second recruit when both remaining refugees are desirable.

Example Boards

P / X / X

  • First recruit hits P: 33.33%.
  • If first recruit misses: board becomes P / X.
  • Second recruit then has 50% chance to hit P.
  • Buying the second recruit raises total success from 33.33% to 66.67%, but usually costs too much compared with rerolling.

B / P / P

  • First recruit hits Purple: 66.67%.
  • If first recruit misses and hits Blue: board becomes P / P.
  • Second recruit is then guaranteed Purple.
  • This is one of the few boards where paying 2000 for the second recruit is defensible.

Quick Tips

  • Recruit when Orange/Purple is shown.
  • Do not blindly buy the second recruit.
  • The second recruit is strongest when both remaining refugees are desirable.
  • Save tickets by planning around free refresh timing.
