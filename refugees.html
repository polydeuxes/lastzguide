Refugees & Recruitment

Refugees are building-buff units. Focus on collecting the best jobs and only investing upgrade resources where they provide the most value.

Priority Plan (Use This First)

  • Top jobs to collect: Diplomats, Nurses, Officers, Butlers, Scientists, and all three Instructors (Shooter, Assaulter, Rider).
  • Upgrade priority: Diplomats first, then Nurses, then Officers.
  • Do not upgrade: Butlers, Scientists, or Instructors. Collect them for buffs, but save upgrade points for the top 3 above.
  • Quality upgrade rule: only Purple and Gold refugees can be upgraded.

Gold Butler Upgrade Reference

  • Base buff: Construction Speed +25%.
  • Star system: 5 stars total, each star has 5 chips.
  • Per chip: +18s free speedup duration.
  • Per completed star bonus: +90s additional free speedup duration.
  • Star 1 chip cost: 5 gold refugee upgrade points + 30 green points per chip.
  • Star 2 chip cost: 10 gold refugee upgrade points + ~60 green points per chip (to verify in-game).

How Weighted Recruiting Works

  • Each run shows 3 refugees.
  • Recruiting is weighted across currently shown, unrecruited refugees.
  • Chance to recruit a shown refugee = that refugee's weight / total remaining weight.
  • Gold/Orange = weight 1.
  • Purple / Blue / Green = weight 2.

Board Decision Rules

  • If a board shows no Gold/Orange or Purple, reroll.
  • If a board shows Gold/Orange or Purple, the 500-ticket first recruit is usually worth taking.
  • Do not auto-buy the 2000-ticket second recruit.
  • Exception: buy second recruit when both remaining refugees are desirable.
