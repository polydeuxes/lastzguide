How the 3-Refugee Screen Works

Each recruitment run shows 3 refugees. When you recruit one, the game uses weights (not flat percentages) based on the qualities still visible on screen.

Simple summary of the footnote: the chance to get a quality is:

chance = target quality weight / total weight of unrecruited refugees shown

  • S (Gold/Orange) has weight 1
  • All other qualities have weight 2

So if the 3 shown refugees are all unrecruited, S is naturally less likely than non-S qualities because its weight is lower.

Ticket Costs and Best Value Strategy

Per run, you can recruit up to 3 times:

  • Pick 1: 500 tickets
  • Pick 2: 2000 tickets
  • Pick 3: 5000 tickets

Cheapest method for Purple: prioritize Pick 1 whenever no Purple/Gold appears early. It gives the lowest ticket cost per attempt and is the most efficient way to farm Purple over time.

Cheapest method for Gold (Orange/S): also focus on Pick 1 attempts across many runs. Since S has lower weight, paying for expensive Pick 2/3 is usually less efficient unless you already see a high-value target and are willing to pay extra to secure it.

Practical rule: use Pick 2/3 selectively (when the board is favorable), but for pure cost-efficiency, repeat cheap first picks across many runs.
