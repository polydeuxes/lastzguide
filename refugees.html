Refugees & Recruitment

Refugees are building-buff units. Focus on collecting the best jobs and only investing upgrade resources where they provide the most value.

Priority Plan (Use This First)

Recruit broadly for core utility jobs: Diplomat, Nurse, Officer, Butler, Scientist, and all three Instructors (Shooter, Assaulter, Rider). For upgrade spending, keep the order tight: Diplomat first, Nurse second, Officer third.

Only Purple and Gold refugees can be upgraded. That means Butler/Scientist/Instructor units are still important to collect for their passive buffs, but they should not consume upgrade points until your core trio is in a strong spot.

Gold Butler Upgrade & Star System

Gold Butler starts at Construction Speed +25%. Star promotion uses a 5-star track, and each star has 5 chips. Each chip adds +18s free speedup duration, and each completed star adds an extra +90s bonus. Early chip costs scale quickly: star 1 chips cost 5 gold refugee points + 30 green points each, while star 2 chips cost 10 gold refugee points + roughly 60 green points each (verify in your server version).

How Weighted Recruiting Works

Each run shows 3 refugees. Recruiting odds are weighted across the currently shown and unrecruited options, using this rule: refugee weight / total remaining weight. Gold/Orange refugees have weight 1, while Purple, Blue, and Green refugees each have weight 2.

Board Decision Rules

If a board has no Gold/Orange or Purple options, reroll. If it does show at least one Gold/Orange or Purple, the first 500-ticket recruit is usually efficient. Avoid auto-buying the 2000-ticket second recruit unless both remaining refugees are clearly useful for your plan.
